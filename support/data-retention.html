    Data Retention & Deletion

    Last updated: October 18, 2025

    EduData Exchange (“EDX”) provides configurable retention and deletion controls to help education institutions meet regulatory, contractual, and operational requirements. This page describes defaults, options, and processes for Customer Data, metadata, logs, backups, and derived artifacts.

    Controller vs Processor: Your institution controls retention for Customer Data. EDX applies the controller’s policy and maintains limited business records and security logs as a processor/service provider.

    Scope & Definitions

    • Customer Data: Data ingested into your tenant (e.g., Ed-Fi/OneRoster/CEDS aligned records).
    • Metadata: Catalog, lineage, quality results, and policy configurations.
    • Operational Logs: Audit, access, pipeline, and system logs.
    • Derived Artifacts: Query results, temp tables, exports, and caches.
    • Backups: Encrypted point-in-time copies for disaster recovery.

    Default Retention Profiles

    EDX offers baseline profiles that can be tuned per tenant by Platform Admins and Data Stewards:

    Standard (Default)

    • Customer Data: controller-defined (no hard EDX limit)
    • Query temp artifacts: 7 days
    • Exports: 30 days (or controller-defined)
    • Operational logs: 365 days
    • Backups: rolling 35 days

    Restricted

    • Temp artifacts: 24 hours
    • Exports: 7 days
    • Operational logs: 180 days
    • Backups: rolling 14 days

    Actual values may be tightened/extended by contract or law. Changes are tracked via audit logs.

    Deletion Workflow

    1. Initiation: Data Steward submits purge job by category, scope (org/program/term), and time window.
    2. Validation: Policy simulator reports rows affected, masking impact, and downstream dependencies.
    3. Execution: Purge performs partition drops and targeted deletes; exports an evidence pack (counts, hashes, lineage).
    4. Audit: Immutable logs record requester, approver, policy version, job output, and timestamps.

    Deletion jobs honor legal holds and will skip protected scopes with an explicit reason code.

    Legal Holds

    Platform Admins can place holds by category/scope. Held data are excluded from deletion and backup expiration until the hold is released. All actions are audited.

    Exports & Subscriptions

    Generated files are retained per your export policy (default 30 days). Delivery links expire at generation+TTL. Recipients must re-request access after expiry; masked and row-level policies still apply.

    Backups & Disaster Recovery

    • Backups are encrypted, rotated automatically, and tested regularly for restore.
    • Customer-direct deletion from backups is not supported; data age out per rotation policy.
    • Upon tenant termination, a final backup may be retained for a defined period per contract.

    Customer Controls

    • Configure per-category retention targets and export TTLs.
    • Schedule recurring purges by term/year partitions.
    • Request evidence packs and deletion certificates from the audit console.
